## üéØ Google AdSense Application Steps

### Step 1: Prepare Your Website
- ‚úÖ Complete all legal pages
- ‚úÖ Ensure HTTPS is enabled
- ‚úÖ Optimize content and user experience
- ‚úÖ Build organic traffic (aim for 100+ daily visitors)

### Step 2: Create Google AdSense Account
1. Go to https://www.google.com/adsense/
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Get Started"
4. Enter your website URL: `https://blessedbump.in`
5. Select your country/region
6. Choose your payment currency

### Step 3: Add AdSense Code
1. Google will provide an AdSense code snippet
2. Add the code to your website's `<head>` section
3. For React/Vite, add to `index.html` or use a script loader
4. Verify the code is properly installed

### Step 4: Submit for Review
1. Complete the AdSense application
2. Submit your website for review
3. Wait for review (typically 1-14 days)
4. Google will notify you via email

### Step 5: Post-Approval
- Once approved, you can:
  - Place ads on your website
  - Customize ad formats
  - Monitor performance
  - Receive payments (when threshold is met)

## üìä Recommended Ad Placements

### High-Performing Ad Locations:
1. **Above the Fold**: Top of content (but not blocking navigation)
2. **Within Content**: Between paragraphs in articles
3. **Sidebar**: Right sidebar (desktop only)
4. **Footer**: Above footer section
5. **Between Sections**: Between major content sections

### Ad Formats to Consider:
- **Display Ads**: Banner ads, responsive ads
- **In-Article Ads**: Native ads within content
- **In-Feed Ads**: Ads in content feeds
- **Matched Content**: Related content recommendations
